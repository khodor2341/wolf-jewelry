Jewelry Care

Keep It Beautiful

01

Store Separately

Keep each piece in its own pouch or compartment to prevent scratching.

02

Avoid Water

Remove jewelry before showering, swimming, or exercising to extend its life.

03

Clean Gently

Use a soft cloth to polish. For deep cleaning, use mild soap and warm water.

04

Last to Put On

Apply perfume and lotions first. Let them dry before wearing your jewelry.
